D&Deasy has been brought to you by the Wildcats.
We adopted this team name as we are not your standard cat, we think outside of the box and survive by getting things done.
Nothing being fed to us, just pure agility, swift thinking and hunger for survival.

D&Deasy exists to make learning how to play Dungeons & Dragons easier for new beginners feeling overwhelmed by the avalanche of information one will encounter.
Let us guide you through simple character creation through a simple, navigational website with immersive training information and tips, which will ultimately get more people playing and having fun!
