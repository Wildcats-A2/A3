MARKETING MANAGER

By Daniel Nanthathammiko

Position Justification

Marketing initiatives are crucial for any commercial product, especially when investment funds have been secured. Marketing is even more critical for a digital product in a sea of products that can lack differentiation.

It is assumed that our project idea is either close to minimum viable product or is ready to ship as it would be near impossible to secure venture capital without details on ROI (Return on investment) and continued monetisation of our app.

Being an imagination-based board game with deep story line, Dungeons and Dragons clearly has a devoted following which may prove difficult to compel them into utilising an assistive application to enhance their game play. This is where a marketing manager/guru would come in.

We would rely on their experience to identify features that would enrich the quality of the game for all users, create a level playing field for newbies (like me) and to help with the imaginative aspects of the game.

I would expect that someone in this position would have experience in marketing web applications and mobile games, search engine optimisation techniques, strategies to generate product interest and player trials of the product as well as have certifications in modern advertising platforms such as Google, Facebook etc.

At my current real-world job, we also use marking tools such as Hubspot for lead generation for our products which is handy. I would imagine in the instance of a SaaS product (Software as a subscription, although SaaS is used with many other acronyms…) we would want someone that understands that we need to convert users on free accounts to premium users that are willing to place their credit card on file for regular billing, providing long term earnings.

A marketing manager would be responsible in ensuring we have a minimum viable product that would provide actual earnings as well as providing continued summarised feedback to our development team so that we could work on the next sellable features.

Company: Wildcats
Salary range: $100k + Super & Benefits
Location: Melbourne, Remote Fulltime

Link to our company profile https://wildcats-a2.github.io/A3/site_team_profile.html

Our company is focused on providing intuitive and functional services to provide a base level of knowledge in real time for new players, immersive designs, and visuals to enrich game play and a deeper context to storyline.

We are an inclusive group that is looking to add value to the company and team by recruiting people that believe in what they say and what they do. As a start-up, we believe in investing in quality, not quantity, which is why we are willing to offer you, a marketing guru whatever it takes to onboard your years of experience, knowledge, and pragmatism to take us to the next level.

As a marketing manager, you will work closely with the product specialist and development team to ascertain the USP’s (Unique Selling Points) of DnDeasy, the strengths and the weaknesses of the product in comparison to competing products on the marketplace. Since Dungeons and Dragons has a devoted following, your task is to identify ways to market our product as a real time app that can enhance the overall quality of the game, for new and old. With this is mind, we will provide you full freedom and blessings to develop product marketing strategies and partnerships that can grow our offerings into new fields and products based off our framework (SaaS, immersive Designs, Assistive Game Play Apps).

GRAPHIC DESIGNER

By Glenn Tang

Position Justification

A graphic designer’s role within a business is to design things for any internal or external projects that could be presented to clients and stakeholders, it’s a crucial area as the overall design can have a large impact on how clients can view the product. Great designs can leave a permanent impact on someone’s mind when it speaks volumes through how its presented.

As we’re basing our designs with an imaginative game, Dungeons and Dragons, creating a visual element to bring these to life can influence how newcomers are able to play the game and allow for an easier time understanding how to make an impact while playing. As there are different ways of understanding how to play, a visual aid is a major tool to allow for quicker learnings on how to play.

Our graphic designer will be the ones bringing their own twists on how the front-end UI is presented to players and stakeholders and designing how the characters look within the application. Our aim is to bring this imagination to life and allow people who are wanting to play this game make the learning process as easy as watching a video on how to play.

The expectation for the employee within this position would be to have a great understanding of the Adobe Creative Cloud or services similar in this nature, the ability to use 3D visualization software to enhance the creations, meeting the standards of any testing and Q&A’s the clients may have prior to launch and the ability to meet deadlines while working within a team format.

BACKEND DEVELOPER

By Carisse Wu Pey Ann

Position Justification

‘Without a solid backend, a great-looking frontend can easily fail’(Nora Kramer Designs 2020, para. 8).

Therefore in this DnDeasy project, the role of a backend developer is critically crucial as we need their support to develop a functional web application. Our web app must not only be operative, it is also an interactive web app, hence we cannot depend solely on a frontend developer.As an backend developer, you need to take responsibility for the server-side of our web applications. Ensuring the web application is well-maintained and functional at all times. You will also need to commit your time and work back-to-back with the frontend developer to develop a more seamless web app for the users.

Most importantly, a backend developer needs to make sure the web application gives off the best experience to our customers that they want to invest more of their money and time.

Backend Developer


Company: Wildcats
Salary Range: $100 - 150K + Super & Benefits
Location: Melbourne, Remote

  • Help with security

    • As for our premium users, since they’re paying for the subscription fees via credit cards to access some advanced services, we require strong backend support to ensure our customers’ privacy is well protected and secured.
  • Add utility to frontend functionalities
    • Require to store data of our customers accurately for future retrieval of customers information of.Th erefore, we need backend developers who are proficient in SQL. For instance, a user login form in the web application. Whenever a user enters his login credentials, the backend code will collect the data and compare them with other login data that is stored in the database to ensure no similarity with the login details and passwords.
  • Greatly improve user experience
    • Ensure the web app is always functioning including performing well and running smoothly with no interruption in real-time on any devices.
